# Trainer Assistant — Content Strategy

## Positioning

Trainer Assistant helps clubs reduce administrative friction and increase training clarity.

Core Message:
"Less administrative chaos. More effective training."

Target Audience:

* Club coaches
* Strength & Conditioning coordinators
* Gym owners
* Sports directors

---

## Content Pillars

### 1. Operational Efficiency

Show time saved by organizing sessions digitally.

Examples:

* "How we saved 6 hours per week in a small club"
* "Why WhatsApp is not a training management system"

### 2. Practical Demos

Short video tutorials (5–8 min):

* Creating a weekly program in 5 minutes
* Using the Day Panel during live sessions
* Logging performance from mobile

### 3. Technical Transparency

Explain why Google Sheets + Apps Script was chosen.
Pros and trade-offs.

---

## Channels

Primary: LinkedIn
Secondary: YouTube
Support: GitHub (templates + documentation)

---

## 4-Week Launch Plan

Week 1: Problem awareness (inefficiencies in clubs)
Week 2: Live demos and walkthroughs
Week 3: Pilot case study with real club
Week 4: Launch + early adopter offer

---

## Metrics

* Template downloads
* Pilot club signups
* LinkedIn engagement
* Demo calls booked

Goal: Validate demand before heavy feature expansion.
